Welcome to Game Night! We're glad you got the invite & hope you enjoy yourself!

This all started with booze, cigars, and darts in Tre's garage, then grew to include Magic: The Gathering online before finally taking shape as the table-top gaming group you see today. Notice the use of the term "table-top" instead of board games? That's because many of the games we play don't use a board! Some use cards, some use dice, some use tokens, and of course various combinations of each.

If you aren't familiar with these kinds of games, that's okay! We have our Library page with a short description of each game & links to various resources to help you understand them. That should help if there's anything you're curious about before game night rolls around. There should also be links for gameplay videos since most of them sound more complicated on paper than they are hands-on.

Even though we have a decent collection of games already, if you have something you'd rather play or think we'd enjoy, feel free to bring it along! We're usually pretty open to new games & themes to mix into the rotation. Don't worry if its a little complicated, Eric can always do the math & keep track of the rules (Dammit, Eric)!
