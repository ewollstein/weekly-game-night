Sentinels of the Multiverse

Style: Co-operative

Players: 3-5

Sentinels of the Multiverse is a comic book themed multi-player, co-operative, fixed deck card game. This means each player chooses one super hero with it's own card deck and they work together as a team to battle the villain, who also has a deck of cards. Each deck is unique and themed to that character. There is also an environment the battle takes place in, also represented by a deck of cards.

Each round begins with the villain turn, followed by the players, and finally the environment. Villains have start of turn effects, play the top card of their deck, then end of turn effects. Players can play a card from their hand, then use a power and finally draw a card. If they don't play a card and don't use a power they can draw an extra card. Some heroes will also have start/end of turn effects. Environments play like villain turns.

The players take turns hitting targets, removing obstacles, or supporting their teammates! Each vilain has a specific flavor & while the general rule is to drop them to zero hit points, some have sneaky ways to prevent or redirect that damage, so the players have to figure out the best way to overcome the villain's tactics! The villain and environment have lots of ways to damage the heroes, too! if all of the heroes are incapatitated before they defeat the villain, they lose the game!
