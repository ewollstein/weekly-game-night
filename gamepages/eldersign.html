Elder Sign

Style: Co-operative

Players: 1-8

Based on the Cthulhu Mythos, In this cooperative game the players are investigators working together to stop an evil cult from summoning an Old One to our world. Explore the locations of the museum to recover common, unique, or magic items, clue tokens, portals to Other Worlds, or the titular Elder Signs. If you find enough Elder Signs in time, you can prevent the ritual, otherwise you have to do battle with the Old One itself to try & stop it from driving the world mad!

Players pick an investigator with a special ability & starting items. As you explore the museum you’ll need to complete tasks to gain rewards or defeat monsters. These tasks are represented by different symbols that correspond to the sides on the dice. Players roll the dice & try to match the symbols to complete objectives on the location cards. If you can finish a task, place the dice you need on the card & roll any dice you have left to try to complete any other task on that location. If you can’t complete any of the tasks, you lose one of your dice, but you can try again with any dice you have left. If you complete all the tasks you collect rewards & return to the gift shop. If you run out of dice before completing all the tasks, you’ll take physical or mental damage.
