Boardgame Geek

Boss Monster

Style: Competitive

Players: 2-4

Remember the old classic side-scrolling video games like Metroid, Mega Man, or Castlevania? This is a lot like that except instead of being the brave adventurer exploring some ruins, you get to be the villain! Everyone gets a boss villain & starting hand of cards. Cards can be either rooms, potions, or spells. Use rooms to upgrade your dungeon so you can lure more would-be heroes in & do more damage to them. Items and spells can be used to help heroes in the dungeons of other players. If you can do enough damage, you’ll defeat the hero & earn it’s points. If you don’t, they’ll get through & make it back to town for the next round!

Different types of rooms have different loot in them. Each hero is drawn to a different kind of loot, so set your dungeon up, replace or upgrade rooms, & try to get the highest amount of loot for the heroes available. Be careful, though, because any hero you can’t defeat in your rooms will count as a wound. Any player that ends a turn with 5 wounds will die. Killing heroes will gain you different numbers of souls. The last player alive, or the first one to 10 souls wins the game!
