DC Deck Building Game

Style: Competitive

Players: 3-5

Have you ever wanted to be one of the great heroes from DC Comics, and maybe take on a super villain with the powers of the Justice League? Well, in this deck building game, you can! DC Deck Building Game is exactly what it says it is. It is a deck building game where each player will get to be one of the many heroes from the Justice League. There are several expansions for this game, so if the original heroes don’t fit your style and you want to be someone else, there are plenty to choose from!

Each player starts the game with ten cards to form their player deck, and they will be labelled as “Kick” and “Punch”. These cards will have a “power” assigned to them. This power is on most of the cards in the game and will vary based on what card it is. You will use this power to claim cards from the main lineup, or attack the Super Villain. Once this is complete, you will discard any unused cards and any new cards you acquired, then draw the next five cards from your player deck. The next player then takes their turn in the same order. This goes around the table for the entirety of the game.

The game concludes when all of the Super Villains have been defeated. Once this is complete, all players will add up the cost value on their cards to see who has the most. This counts as your points. The player with the most points wins.
