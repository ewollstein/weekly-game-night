Smash Up

Style: Competitive

Players: 3-4

Ever wondered what would happen if robots & ninjas teamed up? How about dinosaurs & ghosts? Maybe plants & zombies are more your style? This is the game where you can try all the team ups you can imagine! There are many decks with their own themes & its your job to put any two decks together.

The game revolves around location cards being played. Each player can play one of their minion cards on any location they want. The locations have a threshold number to reach to score them, and each minion is worth a certain amount of points. At the end of each round, count up the points on all minions at each location & if the points on the minions is higher than the target on the location, you assign points to each player based on how many points their minions are worth. You also have action cards that can produce a variety of effects like moving minions to other locations, destroying one entirely, or increasing the power of a minion. If a player gets to 15 points they win!
