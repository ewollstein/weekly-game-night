Agents of SMERSH

Style: Co-operative

Players: 1-4

Do you know all the words to "Secret Agent Man"? Do you find yourself humming the Mission Impossible theme? Ever tried to talk into a shoe phone? Agents of SMERSH may just be the game for you! This has the spirit of the old choose your own adventure games with modernized flourishes in a cooperative board game format. Each player chooses a spy with their own traits & specialties used to determine how many dice you roll on different challenges. You also get to pick 2 advanced skills like driving, weapons use, or disguise that can be used for automatic success in some scenarios.

The goal is to collect Intel on the plans of the evil Dr. Lobo & his henchmen. Thwarting these plans requires teamwork to defeat his henchmen in combat enough times to find Dr Lobo's base & stop him, but you have to use Intel to find the locations of the henchmen, too! If you don't have the right combination of Intel when you confront him or Dr. Lobo gets to his final stage, he's successful & the world is his!
