Legendary

Style: Co-operative

Players: 3-5

Legendary is a deck building card game based on the Marvel comic universe. In this game, you take the role of a SHIELD agent trying to recruit as many heroes as possible to defeat an evil Mastermind and his schemes of taking over the world.

As you start the game you only have SHIELD Agents who give you recruiting power and SHIELD Troopers who give you attack power. You will use your purchase power to buy more powerful hero cards and build a stronger deck. Different heroes have different powers that can give you bonuses when played together. As you progress, you will continue to build your deck and fight off henchmen, save bystanders, and eventually thwart their master plan.

The turns are broken down into 3 steps: Villain phase, recruit and battle phase, and then cleanup. Rinse and repeat until the players knockout (KO) the mastermind four times. The players then count up points based on all of the cards they accumulated over the course of the game. Win or lose together, but whoever has the most points at the end is the best hero!
