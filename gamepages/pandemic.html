Pandemic

Style: Co-operative

Players: 2-4

Pandemic is a co-operative strategy game where each player chooses a role with a unique ability and they team up to find cures for four diseases around the world. You have to balance the need to treat infected cities & the race to develop a cure to eradicate the disease entirely before outbreaks claim the world!

Players get four actions each turn that they can use to move, treat infections, trade cards, or develop the cure to a disease. After these actions they draw two cards for their hand, then play a number of cards from the infection deck based on the current threat level. The regions of the world have different colors of diseases that naturally occurs there & that is reflected on the cards in the playersâ€™ hands. If you can match 5 cards of the same color, you can go to a city with a research station to develop the cure! Cure all 4 diseases and save the world!
