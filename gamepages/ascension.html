Ascension

Style: Competitive

Players: 2-4

Ascension is a deck building game where each player will start with the same basic cards to damage monsters or to buy upgrade cards for their deck. As you buy new cards, they get added to your discard pile so they will make your deck stronger when you shuffle again. Players gain honor points as they defeat enemies or when they play certain cards. Whoever has the most points at the end wins!

There are constructs that stay out from one turn to another, but other cards get discarded at the end of the turn & a new hand is drawn. Some cards will also interfere with the plans of your opponents, allow you to draw extra cards, or gain new cards cheaper. Develop your own strategy to score more points than the other players!
