Zombies!

Style: Both

Players: 3-6

Zombies are everywhere! The outbreak set on the city & you’re one of the last survivors. You know there’s a helicopter pad somewhere & that’s your evacuation point, but you don’t know where it is. It’s up to you and the other players to explore the city to find it & any other tools you may come across along the way.

Instead of a normal game board, this one uses tiles for the different areas of town. Each turn the players go through buildings looking for items like weapons, ammunition, or first aid kits. They can also explore new areas by following the road on their tile until they get to the edge. Then they draw a new tile from the stack & place it any way they want as long as the road lines up with the tile they started on. Keep going until you find the helicopter pad. Once the first player boards the helicopter everyone else has one more turn to get on board before it leaves. Don’t get left behind & turned into zombie food!
