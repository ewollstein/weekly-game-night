Firefly

Style: Competitive

Players: 3-4

Find a crew, find a job, get paid. It doesn't get much simpler than that. Pick a captain, travel to different planets and moons to find work or complete your current job, recruit new crew members, or repair their ship. Collect money & keep your ship afloat so you can stay ahead of the oppressive Alliance & under the radar of the law.

Travel between planets requires playing cards from a travel deck, each one has the chance to have your ship break down, be boarded by the alliance looking for illegal cargo, or demented Reavers who will try to kill you all. Legal jobs will pay less, but be in safer areas. Illegal or more dangerous jobs rewards you more, but are far more risky. There are a few story scenarios to choose from and the first player to complete all the scenario objectives wins!
