Forbidden Desert

Style: Co-operative

Players: 3-4

You’re part of an archeological expedition on a journey through the desert when all of the sudden a sand storm wrecks your air ship! All the players explore the crash site looking for the parts to put the ship back together before the sand swallows you whole! The storm & sun keep pressing on you, so you don’t have much time to find your parts & get to safety.

The desert takes the form of tiles, which get buried with sand each turn as the storm moves. Dig the extra sand up to reveal the tile underneath & find the rows or columns when your parts can be found. Once you find both the row and column, you’ll find the part at that intersection. Get all the parts back & you can leave before the sun bleaches your bones!
